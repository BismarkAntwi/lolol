30 WhatsApp Tips & Tricks you are probably not using

Image by rawpixel.com on Freepik

WhatsApp is one of the most popular messaging apps in the world, with over two billion active users. While many people use it on a daily basis, there are a lot of features and tricks that can make it even more useful. From pinning important chats to enabling fingerprint lock, these tips and tricks can help you get the most out of WhatsApp. In this article, we'll explore 30 tips and tricks for WhatsApp that you may not be using yet, and explain how you can use them to enhance your messaging experience.



  1. Use WhatsApp Web to access your messages on your computer

    WhatsApp Web allows you to access your WhatsApp messages on your computer. To use WhatsApp Web, go to https://web.whatsapp.com/ on your computer and scan the QR code using your phone.

  2. Use the "Starred Messages" feature to save important messages

    The "Starred Messages" feature allows you to save important messages so you can easily find them later. To star a message, hold down on the message and select the star icon. To view your starred messages, go to the chat, tap the three-dot menu in the top right corner, and select "Starred Messages".

  3. Use the "Mute Notifications" feature to turn off notifications for a specific chat

    The "Mute Notifications" feature allows you to turn off notifications for a specific chat. To mute a chat, go to the chat, tap the three-dot menu in the top right corner, and select "Mute Notifications". You can then choose how long you want to mute notifications for.

  4. Use the "Archive Chat" feature to hide a chat from your chat list

    The "Archive Chat" feature allows you to hide a chat from your chat list without deleting it. To archive a chat, swipe left on the chat and select "Archive". You can then access your archived chats by scrolling to the bottom of your chat list.

  5. Use the "Pin Chat" feature to keep important chats at the top of your chat list

    The "Pin Chat" feature allows you to keep important chats at the top of your chat list. To pin a chat, swipe right on the chat and select the pin icon. You can then unpin the chat by swiping right on the chat and selecting the unpin icon.

  6. Use the "Delete Messages" feature to delete messages for everyone in a chat

    The "Delete Messages" feature allows you to delete messages for everyone in a chat. To use this feature, hold down on the message or messages you want to delete and select "Delete" > "Delete for Everyone". You can only delete messages for everyone up to 7 minutes after sending them.

  7. Use the "Forward" feature to share messages, photos, or videos with other chats

    The "Forward" feature allows you to share messages, photos, or videos with other chats. To forward a message, hold down on the message and select "Forward". You can then select the chat you want to forward the message to.

  8. Use the "Reply Privately" feature to reply to a specific message in a group chat

    The "Reply Privately" feature allows you to reply to a specific message in a group chat without starting a new chat. To use this feature, swipe right on the message you want to reply to and select "Reply Privately". You can then type your reply and send it.

  9. Use the "Broadcast Lists" feature to send a message to multiple contacts at once

    The "Broadcast Lists" feature allows you to send a message to multiple contacts at once. To create a broadcast list, go to "Chats" > "Broadcast Lists" > "New List". You can then select the contacts you want to add to the list and send a message to the list.

  10. Use the "Group Description" feature to add a description to a group chat

    The "Group Description" feature allows you to add a description to a group chat. To add a group description, go to the group chat, tap the group name at the top, and select "Group Description". You can then enter a description for the group.

  11. Use the "Group Settings" feature to customize a group chat

    The "Group Settings" feature allows you to customize a group chat. To access group settings, go to the group chat, tap the group name at the top, and select "Group Settings". You can then customize various settings such as the group icon, group description, and group members.

  12. Use the "Search" feature to find specific messages, chats, or contacts

    The "Search" feature allows you to find specific messages, chats, or contacts. To use this feature, go to "Chats" and tap the search icon at the top. You can then type in what you're looking for and WhatsApp will search your messages, chats, and contacts.

  13. Use the "Status" feature to share photos, videos, or text updates with your contacts

    The "Status" feature allows you to share photos, videos, or text updates with your contacts. To create a status, go to "Status" and tap the camera icon. You can then take a photo or video, or type in a text update. Your status will be visible to your contacts for 24 hours.

  14. Use the "Privacy" settings to control who can see your profile picture, status, and last seen

    The "Privacy" settings allow you to control who can see your profile picture, status, and last seen. To access privacy settings, go to "Settings" > "Account" > "Privacy". You can then customize who can see your information.

  15. Use the "Two-Step Verification" feature to add an extra layer of security to your account

    The "Two-Step Verification" feature allows you to add an extra layer of security to your account. To enable two-step verification, go to "Settings" > "Account" > "Two-Step Verification". You can then enter a 6-digit PIN that you will be prompted to enter when you try to verify your phone number on WhatsApp.
